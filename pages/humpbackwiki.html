Hump Back

Hump Back is a japanese rock band from Osaka, formed in 2009 by high school friends. Hayashi Memeko (Guitar/Vocal), Pika (Bass/Chorus) and Misa (Drums/Chorus). They're currently signed to VAP and their major 1st album, "Ningen na no sa", is released in July, 2019. Hump Back is a 3-piece band when the original members were in highschool. In 2013, original drummer Yuuki left the band and was replaced with drummer 373. Activities were paused in December of the same year when original bassist Megu left. In 2014, support bassist Setsuko joined. In 2015, drummer 373 left the band, leaving guitarist Momoko to work by herself. In May of 2015, bassist Pika and drummer Kanna joined and activities resumed. Kanna soon left and was replaced by Misaki.This band is an Alternative Rock Band and they are known for energetic live performances, high appealing female lead vocals thin voice that attracts the audience.
