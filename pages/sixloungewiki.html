SIX LOUNGE

SIX LOUNGE is a 3-member rock band signed under THE NINTH APOLLO record label. Before joining, they had released 2 self-produced demo CD's. The band includes Yuumori Yamaguchi (ヤマグチユウモリ) - Vocals / Guita Oriku Iwa (イワオリク) - Bass / Chorus Shintarou Nagamatsu (ナガマツシンタロウ) - Drums / Chorus. SIX LOUNGE is also known for their high energy live perfomances. Aside from being an alternative rock band thay also adopt some culture of punk rock. The audience enjoy their live performance because of their good interaction with audience, guitar chords are frequently played in power chords with energetic bass lines and drum lines and solos. However as of now the band is active inside the Japan being part of its local music community as well as being an Indie band, the band has a big potential of being big in Japan. The band also played in the Ninth Apollo show with other bands like Hump Back and etc. JpopAsia Source
